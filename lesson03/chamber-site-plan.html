Site Name

Eagle Mountain Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Eagle Mountain's Chamber of Commerce seeks to provide an organization to help promote economic growth and opportunity for local businesses. We provide meaningful ways to showcase local business and provide networking services. We seek to promote our area for tourism and to attract compatible business and industry for the benefit of our members.

Scenarios

  • How do we reach our community in a positive way?
  • Where can visitors get information about our area and membership?
  • What businesses and companies do we seek to attract?
  • How can we partner with our exisitng members to promote their business?
